Biography

Michaela Gyasi-Agyei is a qualified lawyer who is currently expanding her skillset in the She Codes Plus program. She hold a Bachelors of Economics/Laws (Honours) from the University of Queensland, and graduated as Valedictorian of her cohort. She is interested in the connection between geopolitics, cyber security, artificial intelligence and emerging technologies. Michaela was selected to participate in the inaugural Asia Pacific Internet Governance Academy (APIGA) Australia in November 2024. She has also completed Responsible Artificial Intelligence and Introduction to Cyber Security courses offered by the Institute of Applied Technology. Michaela is a member of the Australian Women in Security Network and Women in International Security Australia.
